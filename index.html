About Us

Who We Are

Voicing for the Boychild is a youth-led community action project facilitated by Emmanuel Tanui. The project empowers the Maramoja Youth Group through sustainable garlic farming to generate income that supports boychild education and reduces poverty in Maramoja Village.

Vision

A community where every boy has access to education through sustainable local solutions.

Mission

  • Establish sustainable cash crop farming for education funding
  • Equip youth with modern farming and agribusiness skills
  • Reduce poverty and improve literacy among young men

Location

Maramoja Village, Laikipia North Sub-County, Laikipia County, Kenya

Project Journey: From Start to Finish

Outcomes & Measured Impact

  • Education Support: Revolutionizing futures by funding secondary education for at least 1 to 2 students directly from harvest profits.
  • Economic Empowerment: Lifting 30 young men out of poverty through income-generating garlic farming activities.
  • Skills Development: Equipping youth with cutting-edge farming techniques, from crop management and irrigation to building vital market connections.
  • Sustainability: Establishing permanent infrastructure with a high-pressure water pump and 30 pipes for year-round agricultural success.

Dramatic Transformation

Before Project After Project
Struggling youth group without a steady income source, facing economic hardship. Thriving garlic farming enterprise with 50kg of seedlings planted, generating sustainable income.
Limited access to modern irrigation tools, hindering agricultural potential. Fully equipped with a water pump and pipes for reliable, efficient watering systems.
Uncertainty regarding funding for boychild education, threatening future opportunities. Robust sustainable model channeling harvest profits directly toward school fees and educational advancement.

Sustainability & Future Impact

Through an innovative reinvestment model, harvest returns fund next season's inputs while surplus drives educational advancement. With secured land leasing and permanent irrigation, the group is poised for exponential growth, market expansion, and continued community upliftment.
