Boyhood Echo Organisation

Saving the Boy Child to Secure the Future of the Girl Child and the Community

Boy Child Inclusion & Empowerment

BACKGROUND
For over two decades, efforts to protect the girl child from early marriage and harmful cultural practices in pastoralist communities have unintentionally sidelined the boy child. While girls were supported to access education, many boys were left idle, uneducated, and without mentorship.

This imbalance has resulted in rising teenage pregnancies, school dropouts among girls, and an increasing number of uneducated young men. Sustainable girl-child empowerment cannot be achieved without intentionally empowering the boy child.

About Us

Who We Are

Boyhood Echo Organisation is a youth-led Organisation facilitated by a team of committed individuals. The organization aims to empower the youth through sustainable initiatives.

Vision

A balanced and empowered society where boys and girls are equally educated, responsible, and equipped to build strong families and communities.

Mission

To support, mentor, educate, and guide the boy child in marginalized communities of Northern Kenya, fostering responsible masculinity that strengthens education outcomes and community development.

Our Flagship Project

Mastercard-funded garlic farming initiative empowering youth in Maramoja Village. This sustainable project generates income to support boy child education and community development.
