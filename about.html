About Us

Who We Are

Boyhood Echo Initiative is a youth-led community action project facilitated by Emmanuel Tanui. The project empowers the Maramoja Youth Group through sustainable garlic farming to generate income that supports boychild education and reduces poverty in Maramoja Village.

Vision

A balanced and empowered society where boys and girls are equally educated, responsible, and equipped to build strong families and communities.

Mission

To support, mentor, educate, and guide the boy child in marginalized communities of Northern Kenya, fostering responsible masculinity that strengthens education outcomes and community development.

Location

Maramoja Village, Laikipia North Sub-County, Laikipia County, Kenya
